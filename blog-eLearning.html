10 Academy Quickstart

10 Academy wants to identify, train and enable exceptionally talented young Africans to have an outsized impact on the world.

10 Academy is launching our next edition of training in May 2020 with the aim of creating 100 job-ready data scientists in 3 months, using 100% online training (due to the current Corona Virus situation). Learning will be completely free until learners get a job - the top 5 learners will enter a 6-week custom incubation program to provide them with personal coaching and support to reach their potential. 10 Academy will make its best effort to place all learners into jobs in the African continent to contribute towards building the African data-science ecosystem.

Our vision of thousands of exceptionally talented Africans making social contributions that can go into impact millions of people in Africa and the rest of the world is inspired by the following quote:

The greatest analgesic, soporific, stimulant, tranquilizer, narcotic, and to some extent even antibiotic -- in short, the closest thing to a genuine panacea -- known to medical science is work.

10 Academy response provides unique value in 6 areas for learners:

  1. Connections ​ to currently advertised jobs for top performers
  2. Curated Content​ - All learners can access high quality content that has been vetted by experts
  3. Community ​ - Fellow learners, experts and tutors create a community of learners
  4. Certification ​ - Completion of the course leads to a certificate and a portfolio of results
  5. Completeness ​ - Course provides the complete range of skills needed for a real data science job
  6. Cost ​ - free until a paying job
This next step in the project will be carefully monitored to test out a number of hypotheses and results will be used to parlay into growth of jobs, funding and supporters/partners.

How it will work

Outputs : 10 Academy will select 100 exceptionally talented 4th year university students in STEM fields and by August 2020, provide each of them with the skills, ideas and knowledge to make them an employable data scientist in Africa - we will aim to place as many as possible into jobs. They will be able to launch a production environment, collect data, design simple tests, draw business-relevant conclusions and present the results to the business through dashboards and visualizations to support business decisions.

Learners : Our target group is 4th year students at universities in our target countries (Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, Rwanda, Ethiopia) in STEM intensive subjects (Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Physics, Engineering, others). Through a combination of reaching out to contacts at top universities in these countries and an open call for applicants, 10 Academy will aim to select 100 learners with the motivation of becoming a job-ready data scientist within 3 months of training.

Learners will be expected to have access to a laptop with an internet connection solid enough to facilitate a good chat connection and the ability to run Google Colab, download materials, surf the internet and use chat communications such as Slack.

Users will be encouraged to stick with the training through to completion through the following mechanisms:

  1. Dedicated online support through an online community including a paid community manager, including a focus on using community chat as a central place to discuss assignments and materials.
  2. Feedback on performance through points and a leaderboard system, with differentiated results for top users.
  3. Recognition that training leads to a comprehensive/complete training to produce job-ready talent

Training : Learning will be done using a flipped classroom approach with an emphasis on projects of increasing difficulty - learners will be able to use these projects as part of their profile to showcase their skills. Each week, learners will receive a set of learning outcomes for the week,links to content to self-learn and a set of data with a set of exercises to undertake - 4 ‘simple’ questions and a weekend challenge which requires involved analysis. Leaderboards will be updated daily, reflecting progress among users and publicly showcasing who is leading. The emphasis will be on celebrating progress and group-support instead of competition. An online community will be provided through full-time support from 10 Academy team members. Guest faculty will be drawn upon to create a set of relevant, challenging and demonstrative content.

Post-training & Job placement : The goal of this training is to reach 100% of users to be job-ready by the end of the training. Our target is to place the top 5 users into full-time paid data-science jobs. The rest of the users will be informally supported in their job-search (or freelancing-search) process. Training is free-of-charge for all users until they receive a paying job. Users that are placed through this initial phase will be asked to contribute 100USD/month in the 12 months (max total of 1200USD) after they start their work, with no charge if they are not placed into a well-paid full-time job.
