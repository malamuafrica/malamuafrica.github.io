How We Do It

Engagement Models

We offer the following engagement models to all our customers:

Extended Team

We aim to augment your in-house staff, not replace it. You have complete control over the project. There is constant and direct communication within the team. Responsibilities are distributed among all team members (both in-house and offshore)

Fixed Term Solution Development

from specific development projects to rapid prototyping all you need to provide is the requirement and we will ensure we manage, develop, test and release your product.

Hybrid Arrangement

A flexible arrangement that borrows elements from both the Extended Team and Fixed Term Solution Development Models

From a long-term relationship to rapid fixed price prototyping, we have a numbers of flexible engagement models to choose from. We believe in Partnership. We win together. We offer flexible engagement, 360-degree development, seamless project management, comprehensive documentation, and around the clock support to our clients

Extended Team Model

Unlike the traditional engagement models, we shortlist a number of talented candidates that best meet your project requirement. You can then review and handpick the ones you like the best. A dedicated team comprising of your selected candidates and an experienced Project Manager reports directly to you and workd-as an extension of your in-house team.

You can keep the team for as long as you have projects for them, if the technology or project requirement change, we can even build a new team for you. Following are some of the teams we can build for you: Development Team; QA Team; Integration Team; Design Team.

Throughout the project, we take full responsibility for the following: Managing the team; Timely delivery
