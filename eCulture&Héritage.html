ZADOO

ZADOO is a not-for-profit community driven initiative. As we get started, we are looking for 2-3 exceptionally motivated people to join us to push our start-up effort ahead as ​ Data Scientists​ or ​ Product Owners​ for the period of 1 June to 31 August 2020 (or starting immediately). Further position details below.

ZADOO bridges the gap between employers looking for top talent and brilliant young people in Africa. It’s like Google Search for Talent. ZADOO’s vision is that by getting exceptional African talent into careers, a group of thousands of exceptional young Africans will go on to impact many millions of people.

Building on two previous pilots, we aim to launch our next edition of training (online only) in June 2020.

Who we are

We are a community of people from around the world who want to make a contribution by getting brilliant young people into careers where they can have exceptional impact.

Areas of work

We are looking for two main types of people

1 - Data Scientist - Content Developer (full-time remote)

ZADOO will be a world leader in the integration of:
  1. Data Science​ - Students will learn over 12 weeks by 12 carefully planned weekly challenges. Help to select the datasets, questions to ask and all of the content that goes into making our graduates into job ready data scientists.
  2. Mentoring​ - Provide online mentoring sessions for trainees.
  3. Resource listings​ - Students will learn (in the first instance) independently, relying on a carefully curated list of existing/free resources to teach them the skills they need to get through the week’s challenges. Help to develop our listing of resources to be shared with students, ensuring high quality resources.
Full-time+, remote.

2 - Product Owners (full-time remote)

  1. Community Building​ - With 50 learners learning fully online, the community aspect of learning is essential. Help us to design and build a strong online community of learners.
  2. Post training employment ​ - Our learners will be trained with the skills to be job ready, with skills that match the needs that employers have. Help us find companies that are looking to hire talent, ideally in Africa.
  3. Fundraising ​ - We are a not-for-profit that aims to grow through a combination of income and grants. Help us build on our previous fundraising successes with funding secured from Johnson & Johnson, the Wellcome Trust, UNESCO and others.

We are a not-for-profit organization and at present can offer a small stipend for the 3-month period to cover full-time effort as thanks and to cover costs relating to internet access and connectivity. As funds are raised, we may be able to transition from a stipend to a salary. You would need to provide a laptop.
